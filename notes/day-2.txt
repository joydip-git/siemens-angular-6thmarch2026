Binding:
----------------------
1. flow of data from code to view: data binding
	a. interpolation or mustache syntax

		<element attr="{{prop-name}}">
		or
		<element> {{prop-name}} </element>

	b. property binding
		<element [attr]="prop-name">
note:
 interpolation stringifies the data before binding, property binding does not and maintains the originality of the value

2. flow of data from view to code: event binding
